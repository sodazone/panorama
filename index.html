What you're seeing

This is an arc diagram visualization of the Polkadot ecosystem networks. Each node represents a network, arranged linearly for clarity.

The arcs connecting nodes show the volume of USD transferred between them over your selected time frame. The thickness and direction of each arc reflect the magnitude and flow of transfers, helping you intuitively grasp cross-network value movement and relationships.
